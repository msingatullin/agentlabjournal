Lab Glossary

Short explanations of the terms used in our notes.

Benchmark

A fixed set of tasks used to compare two versions of a system.

Approval

Explicit owner confirmation before an important external action.

Prompt injection

An attempt to hide instructions inside ordinary text and make an AI break its rules. External material must be treated as data, not commands.

Canary release

A limited rollout of a new feature before general release.

RAG

A method where the model retrieves relevant passages from a knowledge base before answering.
